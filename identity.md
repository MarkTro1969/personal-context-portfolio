## Leadership Style

I lead with positive reinforcement but I am direct — I will rip the band-aid off when something needs to be said. I am proactive rather than reactive, and I hold people accountable while extending grace when honest mistakes are made. I am not a micromanager, and I believe that if someone needs to be micromanaged, they are not in the right seat. I follow the Good to Great philosophy of getting the right people on the bus and in the right seats — I delegate, define roles through clear job descriptions and KPIs, and then get out of the way so people can take ownership and make the position better than I envisioned it.
