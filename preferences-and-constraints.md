# Preferences and Constraints

## Hard Rules

- Do not guess or hallucinate. If you are unsure, ask.
- Responses should be in paragraph form unless I request otherwise.
- Do not give me long responses when short ones will do.
- Financial data must be accurate — no rounding or approximating unless explicitly told to.

## Technology Preferences

- Apple ecosystem first. I am deeply invested in Mac, iPhone, iPad, and the surrounding toolchain.
- Build on existing infrastructure (n8n, Claude Code, Slack MCP, Tailscale) rather than proposing new platforms.
- Control4 is my home automation platform — I am both a dealer and an end customer.

## Time Boundaries

I do not keep rigid work hours, but I tend to work mornings — typically 7:00 AM through lunchtime. When in Wilmington, I rarely work afternoons. In Mooresville, the day is shaped around the meeting schedule but still skews toward a morning focus.

## Budget Rules

I prefer to buy outright rather than finance. Even company vehicles are purchased, not leased or financed. Financing is rare and only used when it makes strong strategic sense. I make most final purchasing decisions, but I will consult leadership or finance when needed. For Sound Decisions, purchasing decisions are made jointly with Keith, who still holds majority ownership (over 50%). I always take early pay discounts, and I will push for a better price if paying cash.

## Hard Non-Negotiables

We believe in creating "you-won't-believe" moments for our clients. I do not go against our core values under any circumstances. We always pay vendors on time. We are a service company first — servicing our customers is paramount, and we will not sacrifice quality of service regardless of cost.

## Communication Preferences

Slack is the primary channel for all work communication. I strongly discourage texting for work matters. I also discourage DMs in Slack unless the matter is truly personal — DMs cannot be searched, so conversations should happen in the appropriate channel. Phone calls are fine and welcome, especially for urgent situations. For routine, important, or non-urgent updates, Slack is preferred and I handle those on my own schedule.

## Decision Speed

I am a fast decision-maker. I decide quickly and course-correct as needed. I tend to lean into this almost to a fault — I recognize I could benefit from gathering more information in some cases before committing. I am not afraid to make a call.

## Work Style

I travel frequently between four locations (Southport, Mooresville, Wilmington, North Myrtle Beach), so remote access and mobile-friendly solutions matter. I prefer to understand how things work, not just get a result. I am a builder, not a delegator-and-walk-away type.

## Constraints

QuickBooks Enterprise Desktop is the accounting system and is not changing in the near term, despite its age and stability issues. iPoint is the AV project management system and is the source of truth for proposals and job data.
