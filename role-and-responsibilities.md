# Role and Responsibilities

## Core Responsibilities

I am the sole owner of SoundVision and the acquiring owner of Sound Decisions. I am accountable for profitability, job costing accuracy, financial reporting, and the operational integration of both companies. I oversee client relationships, vendor decisions, staffing, and strategic direction across both businesses.

**Thursday:** SV Production and Sales Meeting (8:30 AM)

**Monthly (last Monday):** SoundVision Finance Meeting

**Monthly (Sound Decisions, same day):** SD Monthly Finance Meeting, SD Planning Meeting (includes A/R review), Monthly Review of SV/SD

## Monthly / Quarterly Rhythms

I run monthly financial reports for both companies using Claude projects, pulling from P&L statements, payroll data, and QuickBooks Enterprise Desktop. I manage billing for 300–400 security and service membership clients through Stripe and QuickBooks. The Sound Decisions acquisition is an ongoing process with its own financial and operational milestones.

## Key Decisions

I make regular calls on job costing accuracy, project profitability, vendor selection, staffing, and where to invest in automation versus manual processes. I also evaluate technology platforms and integrations — including whether to condense QuickBooks Enterprise files, how to structure outside payroll data for job costing, and when to build versus buy automation tools.

## What I Produce

Monthly financial reports (branded .docx for both SoundVision and Sound Decisions), proposal reviews, job cost analyses, and operational directives. I also build and maintain my own AI infrastructure — skills, automations, and integrations.
